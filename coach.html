Questions

Things people ask, before they book.

How is a free discovery call different from a paid session? +
The free call is 20 minutes on WhatsApp. It's a mutual fit check — you share what you're dealing with, I tell you if and how I can help. No coaching happens. If we're a good match, we discuss which program fits. If we're not, I'll refer you to someone who can help.
Do you work with clients outside Nigeria? +
Yes. Roughly a third of my clients are Nigerians abroad or diaspora professionals planning to relocate. All sessions are on Zoom or Google Meet. Payments in naira, USD, or GBP.
I can't decide between The Reset and The Transition. How do I know? +
Book the free discovery call — that's literally what it's for. As a rule of thumb: if you have one specific decision to make, The Reset is enough. If you're repositioning, changing industries, or rebuilding a CV, The Transition will get you further.
Do you offer refunds? +
The Reset is non-refundable once scheduled, but can be rescheduled with 48 hours' notice. The Transition has a 7-day, no-questions-asked refund window after the first session. The Cohort is refundable up to one week before the cohort starts.
Is this only for senior professionals? +
No — I work with people at every stage, but my approach assumes you've been in the workforce for at least three years and have real decisions to make. If you're a fresh graduate looking for your first role, there are better and more affordable resources than me.
