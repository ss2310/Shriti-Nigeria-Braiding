About us

Ten years in Abuja, one address book.

Abuja Heights Realty was started by three agents who got tired of the industry's shortcuts — shaky titles, inflated prices, ghost landlords. We built the firm on a simple rule: if we wouldn't buy it ourselves, we don't list it.

We represent sellers, buyers, landlords and tenants. Our fee is negotiated upfront. We tell you what we think is overpriced, what's a steal, and where the next wave of growth is headed. Then you decide.

"A good agent saves you months of wasted viewings and millions of naira."
