Meet Chioma

A chair, a story, and eight years of practice.

I'm Chioma, and I've been braiding full-time for eight years — trained right here in Lagos, under senior stylists who taught me that a good braid starts with a clean, calm scalp and a straight part. Everything else is rhythm.

My studio is one chair, by design. It means you get my eyes on your hair the whole time. We talk, we laugh, we eat small chops if the day is long. You leave with your neck straight, your edges intact, and a style that holds long after you've left Admiralty Way.
