Meet your doctor

Dr. Emeka Okonkwo, MB;BS, MWACP.

Dr. Okonkwo graduated from the College of Medicine, University of Ibadan, and completed his family medicine residency at the National Hospital Abuja. For the past twelve years, he has been caring for families across Wuse, Maitama, and Garki.

His approach is simple: listen first, explain clearly, treat honestly. No pushing unnecessary tests, no rushing you out of the consulting room, no jargon that leaves you more confused than when you came in.

  • Licensed with the Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria (MDCN)
  • Member, West African College of Physicians (MWACP)
  • Fellow, Society of Family Physicians of Nigeria
  • Fluent in English and Igbo
